Interface Poetry

"We Own The Night"

The Wanted, The Chainsmokers

Design Objectives

To evoke the same feeling you get when you watch a montage in a movie. To make you relive your best memories with your friends/family or make you look forward to good nights. Keep the audience engaged.

Audience Needs

To think about happy memories.To reminisce. To feel positivity. To reminisce.

Strategy

Use bright colors. Use lots of movement with text. Use "happy" and "fun" fonts.
