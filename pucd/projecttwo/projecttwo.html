Digital Editorial Planning

Trend Spotting

March 07, 2017

Design Objectives

To find a creative way to display my assignments/blog posts. Very editorial but also just generally graphically engaging.

Audience Needs

Audience: Mostly people interested in fashion I would say.

Strategy

Serif fonts, editorial looking, image heavy but generally just very aesthetically pleasing. Emulating a magazine spread.

Content Requirements

Street Style pictures, the written material/research.

Functional Requirement

Easy navigation through articles, creatively engaging and pleasing to look at. To be able to choose what article you want to read through a table of contents.
