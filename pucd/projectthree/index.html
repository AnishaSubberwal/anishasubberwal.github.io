Design Objectives

To be able to add features to Pinterest, where you can organize your boards/images based of colors. No matter what the image consists of, it would be easier to organize moodboards. The boards, no matter how they're organized based off Bags, Shoes, Clothing it should be easier to drag and move things to create a new board instantly without messing up what you've saved before..

Audience Needs

A feature specifically designed for art students. Or anyone who needs to make a moodboard for a project, based off pins they've already saved in different boards or more specific search results.

Strategy

To create a color dropper feature, near the search bar. A search bar that has more defined options as well. To also create a color dropper/ more defined options on your profile as well.

Content Requirements

The consisting layout of Pinterest, just slightly modified.

Functional Requirement

Having an organized look, and making everything easier and convienient and put together.
